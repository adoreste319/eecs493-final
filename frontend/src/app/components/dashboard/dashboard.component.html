About Us

Alexis Doreste
Brennan Burke
Keara Bird

We are a group of 3 students at the University of Michigan who built this website for our EECS 497: Human-Centered Software and Design and Development. Our goal was to design a fantasy basketball draft optimizer with the principal purpose of objectively computing your best draft choices at any given spot in a draft. We are here to help you with all your fantasy basketball draft needs, and we are incredibly passionate about what we do! Alexis is our head of backend, and the mastermind behind this whole idea. He is a senior who is going to work as a consultant this summer for the National Center for Science and Engineering Statistics at the NSF. Brennan integrated our back-end and front-end and did a lot of work on the front-end as well. He is a senior who is heading to Grand Rapids after graduation to work for Array of Engineers as a Software Developer. Keara worked on our front-end and UI/UX. She’s a junior who is using her summer to work as a software engineer at SurveyMonkey. We hope you enjoy this tool and use it to help with your fantasy basketball draft!
